A Journey from Kitchen to Coast

In 2001, Muzaffer Koç arrived in the United States and began his American journey as a humble cook in a small hotel. Through hard work, dedication, and passion for hospitality, he steadily climbed the ranks and eventually laid the foundation for Blue Horizon Suites.

Today, a majority of the staff proudly consists of Muzaffer's own extended family and Turkish community members. Their warm service, unity, and cultural richness are felt in every corner of the hotel. It's not just a vacation destination — it's a living success story built on dreams and determination.
